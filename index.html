California on the Edge

This is a project for exchanging stories from Californians on the state's far geographical extremes. Instead of reading about people, let's hear from people themselves.

Anyone can call (530) 212 6073 and listen to a randomly selected message. If you live near the Northern or Southern edge of California - leave a message for others to hear!
